Instructions

Typically two speakers participate in a conversation. Your job is to chat about a movie ${movie_name_1} while playing the role of both Speaker1 and Speaker2. You need to follow these rules: 1) While acting as Speaker2 you are allowed to only pick sentences (as it is) from one of the resources provided: Plot (P), Review (R), Comments (C).
2) While acting as Speaker2 you are allowed to insert some words at the beginning of the picked sentence to make the conversation coherent.
2) While acting as Speaker1 you need to create your own sentences such that it connects well to the sentence you inserted while acting as Speaker 1. Hence, the marking for Speaker1 shall always be None i,e (N)
3) Also while acting as Speaker1 you should create your sentences in such a way that there is clear scope for Speaker 2 (which is again you) to continue the conversation using one of the resources
4) We have provided the first utterance coming from both Speaker1 and Speaker2. You need to continue from there
5) The chat should be coherent
6) The chat SHOULD NOT BE just a collection of alternate question answer pairs
7) You need to use as many resources as possible. Try using all of them but ensuring that there is a proper flow of context in the conversation.

Please look at the Tips and Tricks section

Tips and Tricks

Here are some important tips and tricks that can help you complete the HIT faster than the expected time.

1. Decide on what aspect of the movie that you want to talk about say music, actor, director, cinematography and so on before you read the whole review/plot/comment and pick relevant sentences for the conversation. This will lead to faster HIT completetion

2. Don't waste your time in reading the whole review/plot in detail. You can skim read to get an idea of the movie.

3. Typically, plots can be used for scene description or talking about the story of the movie

4. The reviews are opinions of people and can be used to discuss more about the chosen apect

5. The comments are one-liners capturing . Suitable for replies when you can't think of one or when you want to change an aspect of a movie

6. The HIT performance time will improve as you do more number of HITs. Don't give up if a single HIT was time consuming
